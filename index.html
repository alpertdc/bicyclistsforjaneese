Why Cyclists Support Janeese

Three reasons DC bicyclists are rallying behind Janeese Lewis George.

She Rides. She Gets It.

Janeese has been a bike commuter who navigates DC streets firsthand. Her support for bicyclists isn't a talking point — it comes from personal experience on the same roads we share every day.

Protected Lanes, Not Platitudes

Janeese supports a connected network of protected bike infrastructure — and won't let parking interest groups block DDOT from building it. She's already on the record defending the 15th St NW lane while others hedged.

Real DDOT Leadership

She'll appoint DDOT leadership with a clear mandate to build, protect, and expand bike infrastructure — not leaders who stall, hedge, or fold under pressure from parking interests.
